## 🚀 About Tempo Times

Tempo Times is a digital media platform focused on covering news and developments around the **Tempo ecosystem**.

The platform shares updates, insights, and information related to Tempo and its growing ecosystem.  
Tempo Times aims to make it easier for the community to follow announcements, developments, and activity happening around the project.

Our goal is to provide a **simple and accessible hub for Tempo-related news and ecosystem updates.**
